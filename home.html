Schedule

MONDAY:

  • 7:00 p.m. - Mehrz-audio - Host Mehrzad Farnoosh plays the latest and greatest in electronic music (ranging from techno to EDM, ambient, etc.) and discusses technology news.

  • 8:00 p.m. - My Happy Place - Host Hans Tercek starts the week off with cheer with a mix of 60’s pop, electronic, and whatever he (or his callers) feels like playing

  • 9:00 p.m. - Monday's Muse - Sophomore Alexandra plays her favorites and discusses whatever is on her mind.

TUESDAY:

  • 6:00 p.m. - The Goat House - (Monthly Show) Hosts Nicole and Hans highlight local events, stories, bands, performances and other points of interest near Beverly.

  • 7:00 p.m. - HeirTime - Host Mr. (Jackson) Prince will make you get offa that thang or serenade you until you can't tell left from right or just until you're feeling good. He plays whatever he likes, and defies genre boundaries. This is a free country, right?

  • 8:00 p.m. - Alternative Audio - Alex Legget hosts a weekly mix of the newest and best modern alternative music.

  • 9:00 p.m. - Nocte Solis Illuminatio - Max Newman spins the hippest of hip-hop and occasionally sprinkles in unexpected indie-electro delights. I also hear that he can bench 200 lbs.

WEDNESDAY:

  • 7:00 p.m. - Open Guest DJ Spot - CONTACT US FOR MORE INFO.

  • 8:00 p.m. - Lo-Fi Lounge - Host James Fast plays indie/lo-fi/avant-garde/shoegaze/ambient music from digital files and his personal store of high-quality vinyl records, while taking caller requests and discussing modern music.

  • 9:00 p.m. - Words and Other Noises - During Danny’s variety hour, the arts will collide -- ouch! Host Danny Licht speaks his thoughts on a variety of subjects, with each week taking on a specific theme examined in the episode’s music selection and read-aloud poetry, among other chosen arts.

  • 10:00 p.m. - Post-Everything - Host Nicole Leibman will explore the vast abyss of both acclaimed and overlooked sounds, while discussing the musicians who create them.

THURSDAY:

  • 6:00 p.m. - Matthew and Cameron Make Friends - Matthew Sater and Cameron Flagel make discussion.

  • 7:00 p.m. - BH Jazz - Matthew Shterenberg and David Bruce host an hour of jazz and world music from the forties until now.

  • 8:00 p.m. - 4th and 1 - Arman Zadeh and Omid Shamoil host an entertaining and energetic review of all current events in the world of sports, such as football and basketball. They will also cover Beverly’s own Norman teams, discussing school sports news and interviewing school figures, among other features.

  • 9:00 p.m. - Their Generation - Max Stahl and Michael Simozar play the rock of yesteryear (1950’s-90s), peppered with modern gems. Among songs, they’ll share stories and discussions of rock’s early years.
